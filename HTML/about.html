About The Event

Since 1976, Heart&Stroke Ski for Heart participants have enthusiastically embraced endless outdoor adventures and indulged in the magnificence of the majestic Canadian Rockies. Ski for Heart is a phenomenal opportunity to support a great cause and enjoy everything Lake Louise has to offer at an affordable rate.

Traditional Loop - 4km

The Traditional Loop will be conducted on Lake Louise as it has in years past. This route features 4km of trails and a total elevation gain of 15m. Don’t forget about the stunning views of Chateau Lake Louise and the Plaine of Six Glaciers!

Saturday Afternoon - Apres Ski

4:00 - 6:00 pm

Celebrate a great day in the mountains with our Après Ski - hors d'oeuvres, speakers, awards and a cash bar. Don’t forget to reward yourself with some great items from the silent auction!

See additional activities offered by the Fairmont!

Fundraising Incentives

By raising a minimum of $500 between September and January, you are eligible for a special room rate* at the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise. For every additional $250 you raise, you’ll earn 20 Fairmont dollars which can be applied to lower your room rate even further, treat your taste buds to an unforgettable meal at one of the Fairmont fine restaurants or pamper yourself with a spa experience.

Also, for each friend you refer to participate and who raises $500 for Ski for Heart, you will receive 40 Fairmont dollars. Simply contact skiforheart@hsf.ab.ca, once your friends have registered.

*room rates start at $139, not including service fees or applicable taxes
