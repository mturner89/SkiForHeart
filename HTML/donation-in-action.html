Donation In Action

By participating in Heart&Stroke Ski for Heart, the funds you raise impact the lives of all Albertans, and people from coast to coast. Your commitment to our cause demands the Foundation’s utmost effort to invest donor dollars strategically, funding research breakthroughs today for the life-saving results of tomorrow.

For example, one of the risk factors of heart disease and stroke that you cannot control is family history. Family history can double your risk of heart disease and stroke, which is why the Foundation is backing the best genetic research targeting cardiovascular disease. Today, we know 20-30% of the genes involved in heart disease and stroke, but doctors predict that we could know 100% within the next 5-10 years. That’s huge! And only possible if research continues.

When we raise $100,000, we can provide funding for another research project that has the potential to save more lives. Read about Heart and Stroke Foundation funded research and the impact it has had on the lives of real people.
