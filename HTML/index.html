Join the Celebration with Olympians Justin Wadsworth and Beckie Scott

Ski for Heart has evolved over 40 years, but at its core is the gathering of Albertans who are passionate about the health and lives of their families and their communities. Year after year the event has given families a luxurious weekend away to embrace time together in magical Lake Louise. This year, get involved in three easy steps and meet our very special guests!

Register

Register online with just a few clicks! This will give you easy access to online resources to help you raise life-saving funds.

See where those much needed dollars go.

Fundraise

Fundraise a minimum of $500 to receive your special room rate starting at $139* at the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise. An incredible weekend away at a price you can’t beat. Plus, for every additional $250 you raise, you’ll earn 20 Fairmont dollars!

Celebrate & Ski

Challenge yourself to a ski through the majestic surroundings of Lake Louise or enjoy plenty of other activity offered by the Chateau.
